About Me

Launching his career in his native Middle East, Mr. Boghosian began his work at a local channel in Amman, Jordan. There, he served as Presenter for “Shakw Makw” and “Exhaust”. He handled all aspects of show production, and was an integral player in the shows’ success.

in 2009, Mr. Boghosian served as a New Presenter for the Arab Radio and Television Network. In this position, he presented various new stories, conducted interviews, and wrote show content. During this period, Mr. Boghosian also wrote monthly features for Al-Ereda Magazine.

From 2010 to 2011, Mr. Boghosian was a News Correspondent for Al-Sharqiya Channel, a major Dubai-based broadcaster. Based in Hollywood, California, he presented for news shows, conducted research and interviews, and handled other production functions.

Then, from 2011 to 2012, Mr. Boghosian served as an Intern for the American Broadcasting Company, serving with “Eyewitness News”, the area’s most prominent local newscast. He worked on the filming and editing of various local-interest stories.

Since 2011, Mr. Boghosian has been a News Correspondent for the Middle East Broadcasting Center, a Dubai-based satellite television network which reaches a large, worldwide audience. His duties include covering news stories, conducting interviews, and story research. During his tenure, he has covered a myriad of stories, which have included politics, entertainment, and sports.

Here, he has covered a wealth of entertainment stories, which have included the Academy Awards. He has conducted high-profile interviews with such luminaries as Al Pacino, Ang Lee, and Larry King.

Mr. Boghosian has also covered many Hollywood premiers, which have included “Twilight”, “The Amazing Spider Man”, and “The Dark Knight Rises”.

During his career, Mr. Boghosian has also received many honors, which have included Youngest TV Presenter at the Middle East Oscar Awards, as well as the Iraqi NewsNetwork Most Popular TV Presenter Award.

Boghos Entertainment

Boghos Entertainment is a full-service production company, which services the broadcasting industry, and provides a myriad of services, to our clients who rank among the world’s most prominent news and broadcast organizations. We provide on-site camera equipment, lighting equipment, teleprompters, editing facilities, and other production-related equipment. Our expertise is in all aspects of on-site broadcast production, and we serve many high-profile broadcast companies, which include MBC 1, Al-Arabiya Television, Reuters China, Al-Sharjah TV Network , Dubai TV, Abu Dhabi TV, SAUDI Television Networks.

We cover major Hollywood movie premieres, junkets (one on one interviews with a film's cast), and an assortment of red carpet events. If there is a movie premiering in Hollywood, we are there on the red carpet along side the biggest networks in the world. In addition, we produce content on topics such as technology, politics, health and medicine. Everything we do is incredibly unique, resulting in segments that are engaging, informative, and visually stunning. We like to create 'mini movies' as our content, not just your typical news segment.
